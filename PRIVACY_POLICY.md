# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: August 10, 2025

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the CV Landing Generator ("Service", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. This is an **experimental service in development** running on free tier infrastructure.

**IMPORTANT**: This Service is provided as an experimental service with significant limitations. Please read this policy carefully before using the Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information (via Google OAuth)
When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- Email address
- Full name
- Profile picture URL
- Google account ID

### 2.2 CV Files and Content
- **CV files** you upload (PDF, DOC, DOCX)
- **Extracted text** from your CV files
- **AI-processed structured data** from your CV
- **Generated website content** based on your CV

### 2.3 Usage Data
- Processing timestamps and duration
- API usage metrics (for free tier compliance)
- Error logs and system performance data
- IP addresses (for rate limiting)

### 2.4 Optional Data
- GitHub username (if you connect GitHub)
- Profile pictures you upload
- Custom edits to generated content

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Functions
- **CV Processing**: Extract and structure information from your CV
- **Website Generation**: Create landing pages based on your CV data
- **Authentication**: Verify your identity via Google OAuth
- **File Management**: Temporarily store uploaded files for processing

### 3.2 System Operations
- **Usage Monitoring**: Track API usage to comply with free tier limits
- **Performance Optimization**: Monitor system performance and errors
- **Rate Limiting**: Prevent service abuse and overload
- **Cleanup Operations**: Automatically delete temporary files

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

### 4.1 Automatic Deletion Schedule
**This is a key feature of our experimental design:**

- **CV Files**: Deleted within **2 hours** of upload
- **Generated Websites**: Deleted within **12 hours** of creation
- **Processing Data**: Deleted within **24 hours**
- **Temporary Files**: Cleaned up every **1-2 hours**
- **User Sessions**: Expire automatically

### 4.2 Immediate Cleanup Triggers
Your data may be deleted even sooner due to:
- **Memory pressure** on free tier hosting
- **Platform restarts** (ephemeral storage)
- **Emergency cleanup** during high usage
- **User-initiated download** (5-minute cleanup timer)

### 4.3 Persistent Data
We only retain:
- **User account info** (email, name, Google ID)
- **API usage statistics** (for limit enforcement)
- **System logs** (for debugging, anonymized)
